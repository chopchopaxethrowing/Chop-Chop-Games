üéØ Classic Bullseye

How to Play: Click on the target where your axe hit. Each player gets 5 throws. The center bullseye is worth the most points!

Scoring: Bullseye (50), Red ring (25), White ring (15), Black ring (10), Orange ring (5), Outer ring (1)

üåç Around the World

How to Play: Hit all 12 zones in numerical order (1-12). The active zone is highlighted.

Scoring: Each correct zone hit earns 10 points. First player to complete all zones wins!

‚ùå Tic-Tac-Toe

How to Play: Two players take turns clicking cells. Get three in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!

Controls: Click any empty cell to place your symbol. Winner gets 100 points!

üé™ Target Practice

How to Play: Click on moving targets to hit them. Each player gets 10 throws.

Scoring: Targets are worth 10, 20, 30, 50, or 100 points. Hit high-value targets for the win!

Settings: You can disable target movement, customize how long targets move and stay static, and adjust the maximum number of targets on screen (default: 5)

üßü Zombie Hunt

How to Play: Click on zombies to eliminate them. You have a countdown timer to get the highest score!

Scoring: Each zombie is worth 10 points. Zombies spawn faster as you progress!

Settings: You can disable zombie movement, customize how long zombies move and stay static, change the countdown time, adjust zombie despawn time (default: 20s), and set the maximum number of zombies on screen (default: 5)

üî¥ Connect Four

How to Play: Two players drop discs into columns. Get four in a row to win!

Controls: Click any column to drop your disc. It will fall to the lowest available spot. Winner gets 100 points!

üé≤ 21 Game

How to Play: Click the target to score points. First player to reach exactly 21 wins!

Scoring: Center (7), Red (5), White (3), Black (2), Orange (1), Outer (0)

Easy Mode (Default): Going over 21 keeps your previous score and moves to the next player.

Hard Mode: Going over 21 resets your score to 0!

Settings: You can toggle between Easy and Hard mode in Settings

üí• Cricket

How to Play: Click numbers to mark them. Hit each number (15-20, 25) three times to "close" it.

Scoring: After closing a number, hitting it again scores points (only if opponents haven't closed it).

Marks: / = 1 hit, X = 2 hits, ‚äó = 3 hits (closed)

Winning: First player to close all numbers with the most points wins!

üíé Axe Crush

How to Play: Hit any icon on the grid. If it's part of a group of 3 or more matching icons, the whole group explodes and you score points! New icons fall from the top to fill the gaps.

Scoring: Larger groups = more points. Example: 3 icons = 30 points, 5 icons = 50 points. Optional combo bonuses for chain reactions.

Winning: Each player gets X throws. Highest total score wins!

üé¥ Axe Memory

How to Play: Each turn you get two throws. Hit a tile to flip it and reveal the icon. Hit a second tile and try to find its matching pair.

Scoring: Every correct pair you find is worth 1 point and is removed from the board.

Winning: When all pairs are found, the player with the most pairs wins!

üî§ Axe Word Wack

How to Play: Hit a letter tile to guess that letter in the secret word or phrase. If the letter appears, all matching spots are revealed and you score points.

Scoring: Each correct letter awards points for every time it appears. Bonus points for correctly guessing the whole word. Wrong letters can lose points (optional).

Winning: When the word is fully solved or time runs out, the player with the most points wins.

üòÇ Emoji Frenzy

How to Play: Each round, one emoji is chosen as the target. Aim for that emoji! Hit the correct emoji to earn big points. Other emojis score fewer points or may even lose points (depending on settings).

Scoring: Target emoji = high score. Non-target emojis = smaller score or penalty.

Winning: After X rounds, the player with the highest total score wins.

üèÄ BAD AXE

How to Play: Players take turns calling a trick shot on the target. By default, you call which ring to hit (e.g., "Red ring"). If you make the shot, everyone else must copy it. Miss your own shot and you earn a letter.

Scoring: Each missed copy adds a letter: B, then A, then D, A, X, E. When you have all six letters, you're out.

Winning: Last player who hasn't spelled B-A-D-A-X-E wins.

Settings: You can change shot zone type to include quadrants or ring+quadrant combinations for more challenging gameplay.

ü¶† Infection Mode

How to Play: Players are split into Survivors and Infected. Each round, a Survivor duels an Infected player in a standard scoring match.

Scoring: Both players throw a set number of axes. Highest total wins the duel.

Conversion: If the Infected player wins, the Survivor switches teams and becomes Infected. If the Survivor wins or ties, they stay human.

Winning: Infected win if everyone becomes Infected. Survivors win if at least one Survivor is left when time runs out or when they reach the target number of Survivor victories.

üí£ Landmines

How to Play: Climb the score ladder by throwing axes at the Classic Bullseye. Each throw moves your marker up by the number of points you score.

Landmines: Some scores are marked as landmines. If you land exactly on a landmine, you get blown back to the last safe checkpoint.

Winning: First player to reach the top of the ladder (target score) wins!

üëë Throw Royale

How to Play: Every round, all players throw once at the Classic Bullseye. The lowest score loses a life.

Scoring: Standard target scoring. Hearts show how many lives each player has left. After all players throw, scores are displayed for 2 seconds before processing the round results.

Winning: Keep your lives as long as possible. Last player with at least one life remaining wins.

üíï Date Night Mode

How to Play: Throw at the heart-themed target and try to land in the bonus heart zones for extra points. Play as a couple or couple vs couple ‚Äì your points are added together.

Scoring: Standard Bullseye scoring plus bonus multipliers for heart zones. Optional "date dares" can appear when you hit the special icon.

Winning: Couple or team with the highest combined score at the end of the game wins.

üéÑ Merry Axe-mas

How to Play: Celebrate the holidays with a festive Christmas tree! Hit the presents and ornaments hanging on the tree to score points. Each gift has a different point value.

Scoring: Points are awarded based on which gift or ornament you hit. Values can be customized in settings.

Winning: After all players have thrown, highest score wins.
